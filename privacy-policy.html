GestaHub - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

GestaHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application GestaHub (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In.

Health & Pregnancy Data: The App allows you to input personal health-related data such as pregnancy week, weight entries, hydration logs, contraction timings, Kegel exercise records, journal entries, and appointment schedules. This data is stored securely in your personal account.

Subscription Information: If you subscribe to GestaHub Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync your pregnancy tracking data across your devices
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Improve the App experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) with industry-standard encryption. All data transmission between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing, managed entirely by Apple.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your health and pregnancy data remains private and is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address provided below.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
